There are no universally accepted values — use these as a starting point.

Ratio (obs per occupied cell):
  < 1.5  → grid too coarse

  > 3    → grid too fine

P95 (95th percentile per occupied cell):
  < 5    → well distributed

  → Your value: 7.95
